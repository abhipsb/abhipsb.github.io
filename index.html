About Me

I am a technology leader and platform architect with over two decades of deep-domain expertise in Industrial Automation, Edge Computing, IIoT Platforms, and Mission-Critical Embedded Systems. Currently, I serve as a Principal Software Engineer & Architect at Siemens Technology, Bangalore, where I operate as one of the architects for the flagship Siemens Industrial Edge platform.

Throughout my engineering career, I have architected and delivered highly scalable solutions ranging from specialized embedded frameworks to large distributed enterprise systems across Windows, UNIX/Linux, and cloud platforms. My background bridges the critical gap between complex Operational Technology (OT) ecosystems and scalable IT infrastructures, aligning core software design with strict industrial reliability.

I hold a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA), a Post Graduate Diploma in Advanced Computing (PGDAC) from C-DAC Chennai, and a Master of Computer Applications (MCA). Beyond industry execution, I am an active contributor to the global technical community as a published author, IEEE member, and conference speaker.
